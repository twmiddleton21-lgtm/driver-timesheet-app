## Installation

### iPhone/iPad (iOS)

1. Open Safari and go to: `https://yourusername.github.io/driver-timesheet-app/`
2. Tap the **Share** button (square with arrow up)
3. Scroll down and tap **"Add to Home Screen"**
4. Tap **"Add"** in the top right
5. The app icon will appear on your home screen like a native app

### Android

1. Open Chrome and go to: `https://yourusername.github.io/driver-timesheet-app/`
2. Tap the **Menu** (three dots) ‚Üí **"Add to Home screen"**
3. Tap **"Add"**
4. Or wait for the prompt at the bottom and tap **"Install"**

### First Time Setup

1. Open the app from your home screen
2. Tap **"Create New Driver"** to make your account
3. Enter your name and create a 4-digit PIN
4. Login with your new account
5. Start adding timesheets!

## Updates
